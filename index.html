Why Come Swimming With Us?

Fitness

Swimming is a great all-round exercise. It increases your heart rate, builds endurance and muscle strength without putting stress on the body. It helps to maintain a healthy weight, heart and lungs.

Emotional Health

Exercise stimulates endorphin production. This is what make us feel good during and after exercise. Cold water swimming increases the amount of endorphis produced by added the extra stimulus ofthe cold water. The natural high you get afterwards is unparalleled.

Social Wellbeing

Cold water swimming brings people together in a community. Overcoming challenges and sharing experiences brings people together creating a sense of belonging - finding your tribe.

Disease prevention

Cold water swimming boosts the immune system. It can reduce the risk of chronic illnesses, such as heart disease, type 2 diabetes and stroke. Recent studies suggest cold water swimming, in particular, may protects the brain from degenerative diseases like dementia.
